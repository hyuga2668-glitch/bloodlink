HOW IT WORKS
4 simple steps to save a life

BloodLink connects blood donors with people in need using GPS, real-time availability, and instant alerts — making the process fast and transparent.

01
📝
Register & Verify
Sign up with your blood group, location, and verify via phone or ID to receive a trusted badge. Your profile appears in local search results immediately after verification.
02
📍
Smart GPS Search
Use GPS or type your city to instantly find nearby compatible donors and blood banks. Filter by blood type, distance, availability, and verification status.
03
🔔
Instant Alerts
Donors receive push notifications for nearby requests, eligibility reminders, and donation camps. Emergency alerts reach all nearby matching donors within 60 seconds.
04
❤️
Donate & Track
Chat with recipients, confirm details, and donate — then track your complete history and next eligibility date automatically in your dashboard.
